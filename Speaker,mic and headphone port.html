Microphone port(mic/microphone)

You have seen this pink port at the front of the cpu but it is also located at the back ot the cpu.It is used to connect the mic or microphone to the cpu with the help of the aux connector which is located at the end of mic/microphone.

Speaker port(Speaker/headphone)

You have seen this green port at the front of the cpu but it is also located at the back of the cpu together with pink port.It is used to connect the speake or headphone to cpu with the help of the aux connector you will see at the end point of a speaker or a headphone/earphone.

Line in port(electrical musical instruments/headphones)

You have seen thes blue port at the back of the cpu,if not then you can see it,it is not located at the front of the cpu.It is used to connect any electric musical instrument such as Casio,Gitar etc... They can be connected by the cpu with the help of aux cable and Stereo audio jack(photo is bellow).You can also use the headphone/earphone in this port
